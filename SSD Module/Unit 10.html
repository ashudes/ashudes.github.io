Unit 10: From Distributed Computing to Microarchitectures

WHAT?

This unit explored the evolution of system architectures from monolithic architecture to distributed systems, then to microservices, and the security challenges inherent in each.

SO WHAT?

The shift towards distributed systems has increased the attack surface, necessitating advanced security measures like encryption and key distribution patterns.

NOW WHAT?

The lesson from this unit helps me anticipate new emerging challenges from increased fragmentation through microarchitectures despite some of their benefits. By understanding the evolution of these systems, I can better anticipate future security challenges and implement effective countermeasures.
